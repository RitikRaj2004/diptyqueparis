ABOUT DIPTYQUE

The Diptyque
Advent Calendar

Designed to look like a sewing box, our Advent Calendar is
a nod to the world of artist Lucy Sparrow. Its 25 little
Diptyque treasures span seasonal scents, fragrances,
products for pampering yourself and your home.

-                        fragrances and candle accessories to indulge in or to gift.
